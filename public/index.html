MiLord

Beginning as the leader of a Noble house you must raise tax money, and spend it on hiring subjects to serve as vassals in your employ.

Each person has their own specialty. Hire strong fighters to combat the bandits who raid the area. Hire nimble rangers to explore the region. Hire brilliant adepts to work magics and research new technologies. The choices you make will affect the way your small village grows, and with your clever leadership and guidance you can build a thriving kingdom which will become the capital of a great Kingdom!
